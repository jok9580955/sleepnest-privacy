SleepNest · Offline Relaxation

Privacy Policy

SleepNest works fully offline. We do not require accounts, subscriptions, analytics, or in-app purchases.

Data We Collect

SleepNest does not collect personal information on our servers. Your favorites, custom mixes, recent playback, and imported files stay on your device unless you choose to export and share them.

Permissions

The app only uses local file access when you choose import or export. Audio playback and timers run on device. No account login or network connection is required for core features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. If you export your mix library, you control where that file is sent.
