Support

Need help with SleepNest? This page covers setup, import and export, and how to contact support.

Getting Help

If the app does not play audio, confirm your device is not muted and that audio output is available. SleepNest works fully offline, so an internet connection is not required.

Import and Export

You can export your favorites and custom mixes as one file, then send it to another device that has SleepNest installed. On the receiving device, open SleepNest and import the file.
